
I want to build a game around suspense. One of my favorite games is Little Nightmares which has this odd mixture of darkness and innocence. I really enjoyed the game because the game isn’t packed with your usual jump-scare, gore, or violence to be scary. The game almost feels peaceful and sometimes beautiful… but the darkness and passive storytelling silently signals otherwise. I think it would be cool to make a game where the interpretation is what makes the game scary rather than what is actually happening. This would mean my game would depend on visuals and story a lot more.
