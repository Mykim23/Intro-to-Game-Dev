The game is going to be a top-down 2D story game. The story is going to be a bit dark… about a high school boy that is beat up and abused by his father. To incorporate the suspenseful element, the story will be in the perspective of his little sister, a character naive and powerless in the story. The objective of the game… will be to figure out what is happening. For the little sister (the player) is too young to unriddle her brother’s deep secret, it’s the player’s job to follow through to reach the end of the story and hopefully play it again to uncover the secrets that led to The End.

The game will have chapters. Each chapter will have a short narrative and ability to interact with the characters and objects in the story. For example, one chapter could be about the brother and sister playing Xbox and playing a short mini game so that the player can build a “bond” with the brother. But throughout each chapter, there will be invisible, almost undetectable, clues that the brother is being abused by their father.
